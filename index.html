Electric ATV

In this project I aim to fully convert a gas powered atv into electric. I picked up an old atv that was not working and took it apart completely to understand what was needed and how everything worked. The overarching goals involve fixing the braking system to work with the motor, making sure the motor sprocket required to drive the rear wheels would not snap the shaft, add in features such as reverse and three speed switches and build a 48V Battery for a 1800W motor.

The pictures show my 48.1V battery built with 18650 cells in a 7 parallel, 13 series configuration. It should run for 24 minutes at full throttle, pending tests. The red wires on the top left are for power and charging, and the bottom right wire is ground. I designed and 3D printed the brackets. The BMS regulates current, prevents overcharging, and handles up to 50 amps, while my motor controller uses 33 amps. The side view shows wire connections through a ribbon cable to the BMS, alternating through all positive terminals and connecting to the negative terminal.

Hand Gesture Puppet

This project involves designing and printing a functional 3D hand that can be controlled with a microcontroller and a few servos. Then, using a neural network trained to recognize hand gestures, use a camera or webcam to mimic them on the articulated hand.

The ultimate goal is to make the system independent of a laptop or PC using ESP-32 controllers, which are small wifi and bluetooth microcontrollers. I chose them for this project as they can be fitted with cameras and integrated with recognition software.

Van Helsing

This is a platformer Vampire Hunter game based on Van Helsing made using pygame and python! The you can download the game here and the assets here, just unzip the assets and drag the game executable into the folder and click to play! (Note that windows may detect the game as a virus, just the way the game compiled into an executable)
