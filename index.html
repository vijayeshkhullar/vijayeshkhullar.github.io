Electric ATV

In this project I aim to fully convert a gas powered atv into electric. I picked up an old atv that was not working and took it apart completely to understand what was needed and how everything worked. The overarching goals involve:

  • Fixing the braking system to work with the motor
  • Build a 48V Battery for a 1800W motor
  • Making sure the motor sprocket required to drive the rear wheels would not snap the shaft
  • Add in features such as reverse and three speed switches
  • Wiring along the ATV

    • Hand Gesture Puppet

    This project involves designing and printing a functional 3D hand that can be controlled with a microcontroller and a few servos. Then, using a neural network trained to recognize hand gestures, use a camera or webcam to mimic them on the articulated hand.

    The ultimate goal is to make the system independent of a laptop or PC using ESP-32 controllers, which are small wifi and bluetooth microcontrollers. I chose them for this project as they can be fitted with cameras and integrated with recognition software.
